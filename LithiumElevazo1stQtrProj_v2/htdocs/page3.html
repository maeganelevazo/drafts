Mae's Pet Store

Mae's Pet Store is a small pet store owned by Maria Eva V. Elevazo, the wife of Amor R. Elevazo, the current municipal veterinarian in Kiamba, Sarangani Province. It began during the quarantine period in 2019 that started by receiving orders online, while the products were still placed in a simple shelf. It became known for having an affordable price compared to other stores in the barangay. It then gradually became successful until the store was build. Now, it is still operational and still receiving a good number of customers.
